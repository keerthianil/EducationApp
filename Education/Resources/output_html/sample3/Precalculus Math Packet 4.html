Factoring - follow these steps in order to factor polynomials.

STEP 1: Look for a GCF in ALL of the terms.

a) if you have one (other than 1) factor it out front. Ex: 3⁢x2−6=3⁢(x2−2)

b) if you don’t have one, move on to STEP 2.

STEP 2: How many terms does the polynomial have?

2 Terms

a) Is it a difference of two squares? a2−b2=(a+b)⁢(a−b) Ex: x2−25=(x+5)⁢(x−5)

b) Is it a sum or difference of two cubes? a3−b3=(a+b)⁢(a2+a⁢b+b2)

a3+b3=(a+b)⁢(a2−a⁢b+b2)
